## 上周作业 · 学员作业 FAQ

### 产品不一致 — 提示词切忌再次描述产品

<BeforeAfter
  issue="产品被 AI 重绘变形"
  before='A black dashcam on the windshield, realistic style, 4k...（仍在描述外观）'
  after='Static subject, smoke rising slowly from the background, cinematic lighting change, camera slow zoom in.（只描述状态、环境、运镜）'
/>

**原则**：产品外观用拍摄/PNG；视频 Prompt 只写「怎么动、光怎么变、镜头怎么走」。

### 主体位置乱飞 — AI 可能不知道哪是主体

**空间锚点提示词**：

<PromptBlock title="锁定中心">
Subject fixed in center frame
Camera locked on subject
</PromptBlock>

<PromptBlock title="保持构图">
Maintain composition, No morphing
Fixed scale, No zooming on subject
</PromptBlock>

<PromptBlock title="镜头动、主体不动">
camera moves, product stays stationary
wide shot, subject remains in lower third
</PromptBlock>
